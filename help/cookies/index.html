A cookie is a small amount of data (a small file) sent to your computer by a website. In general, when you visit the KaGaMa website, cookies will be placed on your device that help us provide you with a good browsing experience and also allow us to improve its website and display adds. More specifically, KaGaMa uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Web Analytics. We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics places cookies on your device to help distinguish between different visitors and to analyze how users use the KaGaMa website. The cookies placed by Google on your device are used to collect certain information, such as the date and time that a user visits us, the number of times a user has visited us and the website that has directed the user to us. More information on the use of cookies by Google Analytics can be found on Google’s website.
  • Functional Cookies. We also place cookies on your device to make navigating the website easier. These cookies are used to allow the website to remember choices you made and provide for enhanced, more personal features. For instance, these cookies can be used to remember the status of your login, or to allow you to return to a previous page.
  • Social Media. Social media can place cookies through the KaGaMa website to integrate social media (i.e. LinkedIn, Twitter, Google+ and Facebook). You agree to place these cookies on your device by clicking on the social media buttons to activate them. These social media have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we do not control. Please check the social media websites for more information about their cookies and how to manage them.
  • Ads. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google’s use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to the users of the KaGaMa website based on their visit to KaGaMa and/or other sites on the Internet. You can visit aboutads.info to opt out of third party interest based ad cookies.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. Please note that if you do so, the KaGaMa website will not function as intended.
